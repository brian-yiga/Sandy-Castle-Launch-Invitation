About Us

For over a decade, Mother Goose Nursery and Daycare has been a cornerstone of early childhood education and care in Ntinda, Uganda. We have earned a trusted reputation for nurturing young minds in a warm and stimulating environment.
As part of our journey, we are excited to expand our vision and reach to Kira Kasangati with the launch of Sandy Castle Playground. This new venture is not just an extension but a reimagining of our commitment to creating spaces where children can explore, imagine, and thrive. Sandy Castle Playground reflects our dedication to innovation, with enhanced facilities designed for both learning and fun.
We aim to continue fostering a community that prioritizes the well-being and potential of every child. With our experienced team and enriched curriculum, we are here to support families and inspire the next generation of learners and leaders. Welcome to a new chapter in early childhood excellence!
